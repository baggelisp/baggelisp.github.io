Friday. Web app for home automation

Friday is a web interface that lets you control and monitor your home.
Control all your smart appliances

Software development for automated, eco-friendly and safe home. The concept of Smart Home is becoming a trend with more appliances and sensors getting connected into a single house infrastructure. The aim is to make our living more convenient, secure and carefree by embracing hardware and software advancements.

Services

Security

Smart home applications provide significant security benefits. Monitor your home/business. Get remote access and get notified and video recording if abnormal behavior occurs.

Control

Smart home systems let you easily adjust the comfortable temperature at home directly from your smart phone. Just at a touch of a fingertip, you can close roller shutters, control heaters and air conditioners, switch lights and other home appliances on/off wherever you currently are.

Monitor

Our application allows storage and processing of data in a safe environment applying maintenance measures and analysis of historical data,reports and so on.

Devices

Sensors

Temperature, Humidity, Raindrop senseor and more. Detect events or changes in its environment.

Consumer electronics

Electronic equipments intended for everyday use including lighting controller,sockets, heating/cooling, air contitioning multiroom systems and more.

Cameras

Check who is currently home, access live stream and past events, keep an eye on your home while you are away from it.
